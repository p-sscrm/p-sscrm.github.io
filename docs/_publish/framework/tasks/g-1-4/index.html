G.1.4 Deliver provenance

Task Details

Objective

Enable traceability and reproducibility of the actions performed while writing code, compiling, testing, and deploying software by making transparent the steps that were performed, by whom and in what order.

Definition

Automatically collect and provide traceability data for generated products, including: what entity built the package, what build process they used, and the top-level inputs to the build, such that the data's traceability can be verified and is generated automatically from the build service using a convention common in the package ecosystem. Provenance SHOULD accompany the artifact at publish time, and package ecosystems SHOULD provide a way to map a given artifact to its corresponding attestations.

Assessment Questions

  1. Can you tell me more about your manual or automated provenance data generation process?
  2. What authentications take place relative to the production of provenance data?
  3. How is provenance data verified?
  4. How is the integrity and immutability of provenance data managed?
  5. What do provenance records include, e.g. the initial state of the machine, VM, or container, and user-specified build steps?
