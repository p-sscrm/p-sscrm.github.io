The National Online Informative References (OLIR) Program is a NIST effort to facilitate subject matter experts (SMEs) in defining standardized online informative references (OLIRs) between elements of their documents, products, and services and elements of NIST documents like the Cybersecurity Framework Version 1.1, Privacy Framework Version 1.0, NISTIR 8259A, or NIST SP 800-53 Revision 5.

Our crosswalk mapping between P-SSCRN and the NIST SSDF v1.1 has been accepted as part of the OLIR program. The mapping is the one described at <a href="/framework/frameworks/ssdf">this link</a> and is also available as an <a href="/assets/data/P-SSCRM_to_SSDF_CROSSWALK_20260219.xlsx">Excel spreadsheet.</a>
